Fix Ryujinx after the 6.3.1 Update

RapsterUK 27 Oct 2024
Guide to Fix Ryujinx after the 6.3.1 Update:

WARNING SKIP STEP 1 IF YOU DON'T WANT TO LOOSE YOUR SAVED GAMES

  1. Remove the portable directory (RetroBat\emulators\ryujinx\portable) and create a new one (empty)
  1. Remove \RetroBat\saves\switch\ryujinx folder (if your not sure, make a copy of that directory first and name to ryujinx.bak)

3a. Open emulator (RetroBat\emulators\ryujinx\Ryujinx.exe), it will say no prod, title keys (if not go to the next step)

3b. ryujinx will promt for a update, do it (its safe), no promt? goto help>check update

  1. Download new prod title keys at: https://prodkeys.net/ryujinx-prod-keys-13/
